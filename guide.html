How do I Contact technical support on the NAPPS portal?
  • Go to napps.com.ng
  • On the website, you will find the "We Are Here!" chat widget
  • Click on the chat widget
  • Fill the chat form, then click on the "Start Chat" button
How do I Register on the NAPPS portal?
  • Go to napps.com.ng
  • Click on Register
  • Input your phone number in the number field provided
  • Then Click on the VERIFY button
  • If you are not verified, scroll down to fill the registration form
  • Then Click on the SUBMIT button
How do I Apply for the palliative?
  • Go to napps.com.ng
  • On the Navigation bar, Click on Palliative
  • Input your phone number in the number field provided
  • Then Click on the VERIFY button
  • If registered on the platform, your details and the palliative application form appears
  • Upload documents for each of the palliative information required, then click the "Upload" button
How do I Login into the NAPPS portal as a member?
  • Go to napps.com.ng
  • Click on Login
  • Fill in your email and password
  • Then Click on LOGIN to gain access into the NAPPS Platform
How do I Change my NAPPS Member Password?
  • After Login
  • Click on Change Password button on Portfolio
  • Type the old password
  • Type in the new password, then confirm the password
How do I Add more school branches in my member's account?
  • After Login
  • Click on the change branch button on Portfolio
  • Then Fill the form to register more schools to the portal
